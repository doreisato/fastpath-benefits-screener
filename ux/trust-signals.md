# Trust Signals

## Data Volatility = Trust Risk
Users entering income, SSN, health info need assurance this isn't a scam.

## Required Trust Signals

### 1. Privacy Policy (Microcopy)
**Location**: Above form submit button.

**Text**:
> "We don't store your information. All calculations happen in your browser. We never share data with third parties."

**Tone**: Direct. No legalese.

### 2. "No Account Required"
Highlight this on landing page. Reduces friction and paranoia.

### 3. Visual Authority
- **Government logos** (if partnership exists): HHS, USDA, SSA.
  - Only use with explicit permission. Do NOT fabricate endorsements.
- **Nonprofit badge**: "Powered by [Nonprofit Name]" if applicable.

### 4. Secure Connection Icon
- Lock icon + "Secure" near form submission.
- HTTPS is table stakes. Communicate it visually.

### 5. Transparent Methodology
Optional "How this works" page:
- "We match your info against federal and state program eligibility rules."
- "Our database includes X programs across Y states."
- Link to source data (benefits.gov, state agency sites).

## What NOT to Do
- No fake "As Seen On" logos.
- No misleading "Government Approved" language unless true.
- No auto-playing videos with voiceovers. Screams scam.
