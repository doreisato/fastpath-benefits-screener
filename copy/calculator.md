# Calculator Page Copy

**Step 1: Location**
*   **Label:** Where do you live?
*   **Helper Text:** Benefit rules change by state and county. We just need your ZIP code to find the right ones.
*   **Error Message:** Oops, we need a valid 5-digit ZIP code to check your local programs.

**Step 2: Household Size**
*   **Label:** Who lives with you?
*   **Helper Text:** Count yourself, plus anyone you regularly buy and share food with. (Roommates who buy their own food don't count!)
*   **Error Message:** Please enter a number of 1 or more.

**Step 3: Income**
*   **Label:** How much do you make in a typical month?
*   **Helper Text:** Enter your total income *before* taxes are taken out. It's okay to estimate! Include wages, gig work, or child support.
*   **Error Message:** Please enter a number. If you don't have any income right now, just enter 0.

**Step 4: Expenses (Optional but helpful)**
*   **Label:** How much do you pay for rent and utilities?
*   **Helper Text:** High housing costs can actually help you qualify for more benefits. 

**Progress Bar / Reassurance:**
"Almost there. We never save or share this information."
