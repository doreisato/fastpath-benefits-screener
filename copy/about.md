# About Page Copy

**Headline:**
The safety net is broken by design. We're fixing it.

**The Mission:**
Millions of dollars in government assistance go unclaimed every year. Why? Because the system is buried in confusing PDF forms, broken websites, and bureaucratic red tape. We don't think you should need a law degree to figure out how to feed your family or see a doctor.

**Why We Built This:**
We built this tool because we've been there. We know the anxiety of trying to guess if you make $10 too much to get help. We wanted a simple, human way to navigate a deeply un-human system. 

**Our Promise to You:**
*   **100% Free:** We will never charge you a dime.
*   **Zero Judgment:** Asking for help is smart. We're just here to show you where it is.
*   **Total Privacy:** We don't want your data. We don't save your income, we don't track your location, and we definitely don't sell your info. Once you close the tab, your answers disappear.
